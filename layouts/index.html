StuMeTa — what's that!?

StuMeTa - that stands for Student Meteorology Conference (German: Studentische Meteorologie Tagung). Every year in May, students from all over Germany, Austria and Switzerland meet to have a great time together. The program includes:

  • Interesting talks 🧑‍🏫
  • Workshops 🛠️
  • Excursions 🎒
  • Poster Session 🪧
  • Many social activities 🥳

Find more about StuMeTa in general on the official website!
