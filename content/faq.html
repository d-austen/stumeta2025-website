Frequently asked questions

What is the StuMeTa?

A yearly conference for meteorology students - organised by meteorology students. This year's StuMeTa will be in the last week of May (28.05 - 02.06) in Hamburg.

Why should I come to the StuMeTa?

The StuMeTa is the highlight of the year for meteorology students! Meet meteorology students from other cities, get input about hot topics, get hands-on experience, learn about job opportunities and discover Hamburg in Spring.

I did not receive a response to my application?

Results to registrations will be send out 1-3 days after the registration period ends. If you did not receive an email with results within 3 days, send us an email as soon as possible!

Do I have to pay for the trip to Hamburg by myself?

Yes, we do not cover the travel costs to Hamburg. But it might be possible that your home university can cover the travel costs, ask your local student council!

Do I have to pay for the public transport in Hamburg?

No, we assume most participants have a Deutschlandticket. If you do not have the Deutschlandticket, we will get public transport tickets for you.
